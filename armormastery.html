Armor Mastery

Fighter Armor Mastery adds 3 evasion at level 4 when wearing Light Armor.
Warrior Light Armor Mastery adds 3 evasion at level 1, 5 evasion at level 3, and 6 at level 5.
Rogue Light Armor Mastery adds 4 evasion at level 1, 6 at level 3 and 7 at level 5.

AM(F)=Armor Mastery for fighter classes
AM(M)=Armor Mastery for mystic classes
Hvy=Heavy Armor Mastery
Lgt=Light Armor Mastery
Rb=Robe Mastery
K=Mastery for knight classes
R=Mastery for dagger, archer and tyrant classes
W=Mastery for warriors (any dwarf, warrior or raider class with this mastery)
O=Mastery for orc mystics
P=Mastery for Prophets
H=Mastery for healers
S=Mastery for summoners
N=Mastery for nukers
