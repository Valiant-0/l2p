Debuff Base Land Rates

These are the base land rates of various debuffs. They are not the final land rates and you can't simply rely on this as your chance to land or resist a debuff.

Block Wind Walk/Shield and Mass Block Wind Walk/Shield will always remove the Wind Walk/Shield category buff, even if the debuff doesn't land.

Enchanted spoil for DoT starts out with a 0.00 base rate.
