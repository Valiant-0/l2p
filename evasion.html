Evasion

  • Evasion = ((square root of DEX)*6)+Level+Passives+Weapon Bonus+Armor Bonus+Buffs
  • The final Evasion is rounded down.

Passives

  • Acrobatic Move does not show up in your status screen.
  • It is active as long as you are moving.
