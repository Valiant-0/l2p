M.Def.

  • M.Def. = (Jewelry M.Def.*Level Modifier*MEN Modifier*Armor Bonus*Buffs Type 1)+M.Def. Mastery+Buffs Type 2
  • The final M.Def. is rounded down.

Jewelry M.Def.

  • Simple. Add up the total M.Def. of all your jewelry.
