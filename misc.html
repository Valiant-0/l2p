Miscellaneous Info

PVP Damage Bonus

  • Hero, A and S Grade weapons with an SA increase damage done by normal hits and skills by 5%
  • There isn't an observed damage bonus for having reached noblesse status

Regarding Bows

  • Bows have an attack speed restriction called Weapon Delay (aka the red bar). It's the same thing that forces you to wait on a physical skill to recharge.
    • A Weapon Delay is applied at the start of a bow attack.
    • A second attack cannot be made until the weapon delay from the first attack is over.
    • Formula for maximum bow shots per second appears to be (Atk.Spd./1000)*(1500/Weapon Delay)
    • Normal bows have a base Weapon Delay of 1500
    • Yumi bows have a base Weapon Delay of 820
    • Standing still and with no SA, normal bows and yumi bows shoot the exact same number of shots per second.
    • Normal Bows allow faster use of skills and more kiteability due to having a higher Atk. Spd. while Yumi Bows have significant P.Atk.

Cancel

  • Pre-Interlude Cancel is (Learn Level/Buff Level), which is why enchanted buffs will turn 76+ and will be harder to resist.
  • Interlude Cancel works the same as Sorcerer's Cancel. It is resisted by skills such as Lionheart and Arcane Protection. If no Cancel Resistance is provided, the last 5 buffs will be removed, otherwise it will be calculated against Cancel Resistance and check to the next buff if Resistance is granted.
