    Critical Damage

    • Crt. Damage from the weapon SA or passive of the same name and from Vicious Stance are calculated seperately from the rest.
      • They are not boosted by critical damage buffs, boss jewelry, etc.
      • They are not boosted by soulshots.
    Average Critical Damage(Base Damage)+(Crt. Damage)
    Maximum Critical Damage((Base Damage)+(Crt. Damage))*(Maximum Damage Modifier)
    Minimum Critical Damage((Base Damage)+(Crt. Damage))*(Minimum Damage Modifier)
