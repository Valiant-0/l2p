Some sorta random explanations:

The summons' base stats affect their resistances and such. They also affect a lot of things you can't change anyway because you can't give a summon a tattoo, but ok.

Learn level is the level the game considers the skill "learned" at even though you got your summon at a different level. It affects several things, including what level recharge starts to weaken at or how well debuffs land.

Lastly, I've got to say this: NCSoft is very, very lazy when it comes to making summon stats. It doesn't really seem like they wanted summoners to be "hard" to level with because of crappy summon stats. No, they were just too lazy to change the summon stats any more than they had to.
