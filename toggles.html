Q. Why doesn't it add as much as you said it would on my status window screen?

They don't work that way exactly. Oh yes, it does add what I say it does. Just not the way it seems like it would. Confused? Good. Look here and follow the links for whatever stat you're looking at.

Q. It says the "blah blah HP or MP per tick" is this, but in game it's totally different. Why!

The numbers you'll see here are just base values. I do not know the formula for per tick stuff at present. The only exception should be the % changes. Percent changes should be correct as listed but it's still confusing because of the regen formulas.
