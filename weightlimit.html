Weight Limit

  • Weight Limit = (CON Modifier*69000)*Skills
  • The final Weight Limit is rounded down.
  • Buffs and Equipment Bonuses like Reduce Weight do not increase Weight Limit. They increase the threshold of mp regen reduction due to weight.
